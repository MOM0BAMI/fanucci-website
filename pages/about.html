About Fanucci

Our Story

Fanucci is a family‑controlled business established in 1983. At the outset of our journey, our primary focus was on engineering and industrial fasteners. As the company grew, we recognised a significant demand for both domestic and industrial electrical components.

Today, we are perfectly positioned to meet any demand brought to our doorstep, well into the future. Our experienced wholesale and retail sales teams are experts at procuring products both locally and internationally. They are dedicated to assisting you with all your requirements, ensuring your project is a success by utilising the correct products and brands.
